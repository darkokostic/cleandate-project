How It Works

It’s simple – follow the easy steps provided with the kit, send our lab your samples & check online in 5 days to view, save or delete your results.

Self Collection

Select the testing kit that’s right for you, the team at GetTested will ship your kit to the address of your choice in discreet packaging. Each kit contains easy to follow instructions, self collection is simple and only takes a few minutes.

Return your samples

Once you’ve collected your samples, put them back inside the box and ship your entire kit to our laboratory with the FEDEX prepaid shipping bag that is included in your kit.

Get results online

Our Lab will analyze your samples and post your results online using a secure HIPAA-compliant portal. You’ll receive an email with instructions on how you can retrieve your results via the private portal.
